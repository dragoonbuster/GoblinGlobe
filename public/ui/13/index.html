What is a Domain?
A domain is the name people type to visit your website, like "google.com" or "disney.com"!
Domain Extensions
The part after the dot (.com, .net, .org) is called an extension. Each one has a special meaning!
Good Domain Tips
Great domains are short, easy to remember, and describe what your website is about!
